SMS Messaging Program

How CallLocally uses text messaging — last updated April 17, 2026

Summary: When you call a business that uses CallLocally and the business doesn't answer, CallLocally sends you a follow-up text on the business's behalf so they can still help. This page explains how that messaging works, your rights, and how to opt out.

1. How You Enroll (Call-to-Action)

You are enrolled in the SMS program by calling a business that uses CallLocally. There is no separate sign-up form, no keyword, and no web checkbox — the inbound phone call itself is how you express interest in being contacted by that business.

By placing that call, you acknowledge that if the business does not answer, CallLocally may send you a text message from the business to follow up on your inquiry.

2. What Messages You Will Receive

All messages sent through CallLocally are transactional — they relate to the specific inquiry you initiated. You will never receive marketing, promotional, or cold-outreach messages through this program.

Typical messages include:

"Hi! This is Gustavos Plumbing — sorry I missed your call. What's the plumbing issue and your address? (leak, clog, no hot water) I'll text back ASAP with timing & pricing. Reply STOP to opt out."
"Thanks! Gustavos Plumbing got your message and will text back shortly with timing and pricing. Reply STOP to opt out."
"On my way — should be there in about 30 minutes. Estimate is $250 for the leak repair, final price after I see it. Reply STOP to opt out."
"Confirming your appointment tomorrow at 9am at 505 35th St Apt A. Text this number if anything changes. Reply STOP to opt out."

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies with your inquiry. Typically 1 to 5 messages per service request. You will not receive messages from a business you haven't called.

4. Costs

Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier's plan. CallLocally and the business do not charge you to send or receive these messages, but your wireless carrier may.

5. How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying one of the following keywords to any message:

STOP   UNSUBSCRIBE   CANCEL   END   QUIT

You will receive one final confirmation message and will not receive any further SMS from that business through CallLocally. To resubscribe, reply START.

6. How to Get Help

Reply HELP or INFO to any message to receive support contact information. You can also email support@calllocally.com at any time.

7. Privacy & Data Use

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in status with third parties for marketing purposes. Your number is used only to deliver the transactional text messages described above.

For full details on how we collect, use, and retain information, see our Privacy Policy.

8. Supported Carriers

CallLocally supports SMS delivery to all major U.S. mobile carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, US Cellular, and most MVNOs. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
