FAQ

Common questions.

Do my customers need to do anything different?
Nope. They keep calling the exact same number they always have. The forwarding happens invisibly on your phone your customers never know anything changed.
What if I answer the call? Will they still get a text?
No. CallLocally only fires when a call goes unanswered. If you pick up, everything works exactly as it always did. You set the forwarding to "unanswered calls only" during setup.
How long does it take before leads actually come through?
You're live in 5 minutes as soon as you set up call forwarding, missed calls trigger texts. Your toll-free number goes through a 57 day carrier verification for full deliverability. Leads come through during that time too, just at standard throughput.
What number do my customers see when they get texted?
They'll see your dedicated CallLocally toll-free number. You can customize the message to say your business name so they immediately know who it's from.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, anytime. If you cancel, you keep service through the end of your billing period. No fees, no hoops.
What trades does this work for?
Any home service trade plumbers, HVAC techs, electricians, roofers, landscapers, pest control, handymen, painters, pool service, and more. If you miss calls, CallLocally helps.
